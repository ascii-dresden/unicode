What is all this really about?

We are a bunch of actual geeks, we hang out at a place called ascii which is an actual café, residing in the APB of the TU-Dresden. We grew tired of being asked why that place is called "ascii" and not "unicode" or "utf8" so we made up this fake hipster coffee-joint to be able to blame them for already having taken the name. Come visit us and say hello. We do have sofas :D
